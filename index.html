About Me

My name is MohammadReza Modarres. I was born in Tabriz, Iran. I accomplished my education on bachelor's of Mechanical Engineering/Fluids and Heat at University of Tabriz on Mechanical Engineering Campus in 2010. The subject of my project was simulation of O.457 engine, which is one the diesel engines has been produced by the Mercedes-Benz company. Comparing outputs of the software with the experimental data was our method to analyze and validate our data. The final part of my project was proposing remedies for declining the pressure wave frequencies in order to lessen the diesel-knocks produced along with combution phenomena in compression ignition engines. As I was interested in Gas-turbines so I used my willpower to change my field of study from Mechanical Engineering to Aerospace Engineering. I did change my field of study from Mechanical Engineering to Aerospace Engineering to fulfil one of my intrinsic interests even more.

Coming to Sharif University of Technology on Sep. 2011. I passed 24 credits relevant to my field of study. By then, I have engaged with industrial project about Waste Gas Incinerators since Sep.2013. Nowadays I am working on Chemical Reactor Networks to estimate the amounts of produced byproducts of combustion phenomena which it takes too much for a researcher to predict these amount by means of Computational Fluid Dynamics along with Reacting Flow Solution of the problem. So, proposing this remedy could be more efficient and more expeditiously than solutions of these two problems together with each other. I believe that the milieu which is made by gathering Iranian Exceptional talents in Sharif University of Technology cause me to grow up more wheter intellectually or scientifically.
